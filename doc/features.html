cvnirc-cli specific

  • For switching channels in the GNU readline-based command-line interface, press ^O (that is, Ctrl-O). This is mostly the same as what Irssi does with ^X. It's possible to supply an "argument" or "count" to the cycle-context function, e.g., press Alt-- ^X (that is, Alt-minus or Esc minus followed by Ctrl-X) to go one step backwards instead of forward.
